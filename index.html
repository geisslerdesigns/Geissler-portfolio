Tina Geissler · Designer

I grew up in Germany, where I trained as an elementary school teacher, which turned out to be the best design education I ever got. If you can explain something to a seven-year-old, you can explain it to anyone.

In 2020 I moved to the US as an au pair, fell in love with building things, and ended up graduating in 2025 with two associate's degrees. Graphic Design and Computer Science. I can design it and build it. No handoffs, and therefore nothing lost in translation.

I'm building Geissler Designs because I genuinely love watching a project go from a vague idea to something someone is proud to show off.
